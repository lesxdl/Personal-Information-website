About Classic Watch

Classic Watch

This watch are integrated and independent classic watch manufacture. Headquartered in Jupiter, it is recognized for its expertise and the quality of its products.

Brent Camero, founder of the company, instilled a notion of perpetual excellence, leading to major watchmaking innovations, It is a committed and responsible company whose products are made to last. In its quest for excellence, Classic Watch strives each day to improve not only its watches but also its environmental performance and its impact on society.
